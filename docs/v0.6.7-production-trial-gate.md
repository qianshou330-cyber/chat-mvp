## Chrome Production Smoke Checklist

Run this before inviting 20-30 company trial users. Use real trial accounts or temporary `codex.v067.*@example.com` accounts, then clean temporary data with `docs/test-data-cleanup-sop.md`.

- Register or sign in with email/password.
- Confirm chat list, direct chat, group chat, and current-conversation search.
- Send text, image, file, and video messages.
- Open image preview and video card playback.
- Confirm group details: invite instructions, member list, group files, group mute, member mute, group management records, and trial inspection.
- Confirm member cannot see owner/admin-only management records or actions.
- Confirm non-member cannot read private chat, group messages, attachments, group files, push subscriptions, or management records.
- Confirm Web Push: member B enables notifications, member A sends a message, B receives the generic notification without message body, attachment filename, or email.
- Confirm login devices: same account in two browsers appears as two devices; `退出其他设备` keeps current device signed in and signs out the other after refresh or heartbeat.

## 20-30 Person Trial Start Checklist

- Pick one owner/admin as the trial operator.
- Ask all participants to register on `https://chat-mvp-tau.vercel.app`.
- Owner/admin adds members from group details by registered email.
- Share `docs/company-trial-safety.md` with participants before use.
- Use GitHub Issues for all feedback and classify P0/P1/P2/P3.
- P0/P1 fixes must stay minimal: stability, permissions, notification, attachment, member management, or mobile blocking issues only.
